NutritionLab

Prototype in Development

NutritionLab is a prototype of a community nutrition laboratory currently in research and design phase. The project aims to develop an inclusive approach to address nutritional needs of different vulnerable groups: children, families, elderly people, and pregnant women.

Key Objectives

  • Develop an accessible nutrition analysis system adapted to local context
  • Create fortified food production solutions for different target groups
  • Train local actors in nutritional screening and personalized counseling
  • Establish a monitoring and evaluation framework to measure nutritional impact

Beneficiary tracking, impact indicators, applied research

Current Status

🔬 Research Phase 📐 Design in Progress ⏳ Pending Implementation

The conceptual prototype is currently under development. The project is actively seeking technical partnerships and funding opportunities to move to the pilot phase.

Vision & Potential

NutritionLab represents an initiative with strong social potential, designed to be adaptable to school, community, and family contexts. The inclusive, evidence-based approach aims to create lasting impact on the nutritional health of vulnerable populations.

Ceramic Water Filter Initiative

Water Safety Project

This initiative develops ceramic water filters to provide safe drinking water to rural communities. It combines environmental sustainability with water hygiene practices to ensure long-term health benefits.

Key Objectives

  • Design and produce affordable ceramic water filters locally
  • Train communities on proper water treatment techniques
  • Reduce waterborne diseases in rural households
  • Promote sustainable water management practices
